About

I'm a Data and Analytics Engineer with 5+ years building the data infrastructure that powers business decisions - from scalable ETL pipelines and cloud data platforms, to enterprise BI dashboards that leadership teams actually depend on daily.

At USAA, I built and modernized enterprise-scale data pipelines, self-serve reporting platforms, and executive dashboards adopted across the organization. Prior to that, at PricewaterhouseCoopers, I led Salesforce data architecture and API integrations for large Healthcare and Energy clients. At Dick's Sporting Goods, I built large-scale data pipelines enabling downstream ML and analytics for Loss Prevention.

I hold an MS in Business Analytics from the McCombs School of Business, UT Austin and a B.Tech in Computer Science from Manipal Institute of Technology. I specialize in building data systems that are reliable, scalable, and actually used by the people they're built for.

My work spans the full data lifecycle - from raw ingestion and transformation to modeling, deployment, and executive reporting. I bring both the technical depth to build production-grade pipelines and the stakeholder fluency to translate complex data into decisions that matter.

I hold 9 industry certifications including Snowflake SnowPro Core and 7 Salesforce credentials. I have presented research at the RILA and LPRC national industry conferences and won the firm-wide PwC Architectural Review Board White Paper Contest.

Currently based in San Antonio, TX. Open to data engineering, analytics engineering, and BI roles across industries.
